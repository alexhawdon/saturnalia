Mission Statement

Summer Saturnalia is a non-profit event dedicated to providing both a platform for talented musicians and artists, and a friendly, welcoming environment for like-minded people to come together and appreciate these works.

We are committed to providing a truly unique experience which is facilitated by close co-operation with contributors and guests alike: everyone is involved.
