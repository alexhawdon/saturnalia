Saturnalia Snacks

Supplying cooked breakfasts, gourmet burgers/hotdogs, sandwiches and other lunchtime sundries, teas and coffees, and something delicious and home-cooked in the evening. Weather permitting, a Saturnalia BBQ will be happening on the Sunday!

Saturnalia Paraphernalia

Summer Saturnalia HQ will feature a small shop selling various essentials: cigs/tobacco; official Summer Saturnalia t-shirts (accept no cheap imitations from the touts outside!), batteries and stuff; sweets and paracetamols to sooth fuzzy heads; and a few surprises, both practical and totally impractical!

The Basics

More toilets than we need, serviced throughout the weekend.

Free water, as per any big event like this - gotta brush your teeth (please!)

Mobile phone charging - located in Hyperion, just a point to plug in for awhile while you relax to to audio/visual treats.

First-Aiders and First Aid point, available throughout the weekend.

Summer Saturnalia Spaceguard: our friendly security team keeping the outside world where it should be - out!

Volunteers from Barnardos will be available for assistance.
