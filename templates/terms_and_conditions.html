Terms and Conditions

TICKETS ARE STRICTLY NON-TRANSFERABLE

Age restrictions: Strictly 18 years and over. Ticket touting and selling on eBay, chat forums, websites or other 'auctions' is prohibited. All fees will be charged in (£) pounds sterling (GBP).

PURCHASER SECURITY

All transactions are processed by Google, your credit card information is handled and secured by Google at all time.

E-Tickets

All ticketing is fully electronic, so no printed tickets or forms will be posted out. Print your ticket and bring to the festival to exchange for a wrist band. The location will be printed on your ticket.

Deposits

A deposit payment is £8 (includes £2.50 booking fee) . You have until 01/07/11 to pay the remaining balance in one single payment. To pay the remaining balance you will need to enter your deposit code, which was emailed to you from both Google Checkout and Summer Saturnalia. Failure to settle the financial balance by the 01/07/11 will result in the forfeiting of your deposit amount.

Refunds

Refunds will only be given in the unlikely circumstances that events are cancelled, or if Summer Saturnalia has been negligent or acted in breach of contract. However, refunds will not be given if events are cancelled due to 'Acts of God' and reasons including the following: Terrorism / Biological action, Foot and Mouth, Data Problems, Breach of License, SARS, National Mourning for anyone over 65 years old, Avian Flu, Swine Flu and European Pension Reform, Labour disputes or disruption caused by volcano or other similar natural phenomena.
Any refunds given by Summer Saturnalia will be for the face value of tickets sold and will not include the booking fee. All booking fees are non-refundable. No refunds will be given to any persons who are ejected for behaving offensively or carrying such items or substances as deemed prohibited by law.

Privacy policy

Summer Saturnalia does not and will not have access to your financial details. They will only be made available to Google. We will not share your personal information for marketing or any other purposes without your consent unless where required to by law. We will always respect your privacy and any personal communication between you and ourselves. We will always comply with any data protection legislation currently in force.
Your email address will be added to the Summer Saturnalia’s emailing list. You will receive updates about the Summer Saturnalia Festival and other future Summer Saturnalia events. You can change or unsubscribe your email at any time by emailing us with ‘Unsubscribe’ as the subject heading.
The lineup of Summer Saturnalia is subject to change.

Ambassador policy

All ambassadors must have paid a ticket deposit of £8 (includes £2.50 booking fee) prior to ambassador sign up. The remaining £49.50 will be waivered once the ambassador has reached their required target. Guest ambassadors must sell 10 tickets by having their guests use your unique ambassador code as a payment reference. Solo contributors must sell 3 tickets in the same way. Group ambassadors are required to sell 6 tickets. All tickets must be purchased before 01/07/2011 to qualify for the fee waiver.

Admissions policy

Your ticket will be checked before access is allowed to the camping area. Once you enter the arena your ticket’s bar-code will be checked against the database and you will receive your wrist band. Nobody without a valid ticket will be admitted, and there will be no entry after midnight. Right of admission reserved (R.O.A.R.). Summer Saturnalia is for nice people and is about having a good time. Anti social behaviour will not be tolerated and people behaving offensively will be ejected without a refund. Guests may be searched on entry to the event. If you are in possession of illegal substances you may be arrested.
EVERY PERSON ON SITE IS REQUIRED TO HAVE A TICKET SCANNED TO GAIN ENTRY
Contributors and contractors will have their ticket emailed out to them before the event.

Photography and Recording

Ticket holders give their express consent to the use of the actual or simulated likeness in connection with the production, exhibition, advertising and/or exploitation of any film, video and/or audio recording of this event and/or any element thereof in any/all media throughout the world.

Alcohol

You are allowed to bring in your own alcohol (for consumption in the camping field) but only a reasonable amount per person. You will be asked to leave excess alcohol outside the event. Once you have entered the event you cannot re-enter with more alcohol supplies. Alcohol cannot be brought into the main site from the camp site. There are fully licensed bars available for you.

Glass

You will not be allowed to bring in glass bottles or any kind. Broken glass is dangerous for animals who will be using the fields after the event. Leave all glass in the car, decant it into plastic bottles or bin it. There are no exceptions.

Parking

There is a designated car park. No cars allowed in either the arena or camping areas. Caravans, Trailer Tents and anything else that needs to be pulled in and out by a car are not permitted.

Fires

We have a no naked flame policy at the festival and in the camp site. Fires, flares, fireworks will result in you been ejected without refund.

Animals and Pets

No pet animals are allowed on the festival site other than dogs assisting the visually impaired.
